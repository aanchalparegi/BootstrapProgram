The surroundings we live in, the food we eat, the water we drink and the air we breathe in are all part of nature. Nature is the primary source of nutrition and nourishment for all living creatures on the earth. Water and oxygen are the essential things for all, and nature has provided us with plenty of both.Essentially, nature is everything we are surrounded by like the water we drink, the air we breathe, the sun we soak in, the birds we hear chirping, the moon we gaze at and more. Above all, it is rich and vibrant and consists of both living and non-living things.
